Project Highlights

  • Conducted comprehensive research on the impacts of emergency service work on children's mental health and wellbeing
  • Led focus groups with 23 children of emergency service workers to understand their experiences
  • Analyzed qualitative data to identify key themes and patterns in children's experiences
  • Developed the A.C.E. framework for supporting children of emergency service workers
  • Created recommendations for improving support systems and resources
